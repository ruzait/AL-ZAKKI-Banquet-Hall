About Us

Why Choose Al-Zakki?

Where Every Celebration Becomes a Memory — Elegance, Comfort, Flexibility

5+ Years of Excellence

Welcoming Banquet Hall

AL-ZAKKI BANQUET HALL is the biggest, most friendly, and flexible banquet hall located on Addalaichenai Main Road — well-known and easily accessible for everyone. Established in 2020, we proudly offer 5+ years of excellence in hosting memorable celebrations and gatherings.

  • Spacious 3-floor venue with capacity up to 3000 guests
  • Basic sound system with minimal stage lighting
  • Dedicated bridal suite & prayer area
  • Nearby parking available for 50+ vehicles
  • Flexible & affordable packages for all budgets
